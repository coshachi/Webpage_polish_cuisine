Bigos

Bigos is a pickled cabbage and meat based dish. It is often a best way to utilise leftover hams, sausages and bacon. It is quite laborious in preparation, however the effort is definelly rewarded (...)

Boiled cabbage rolls

Boiled cabbage rolls are most commonly stuffed with minced pork and rice, however that can also be stuffed with other kinds of mince meat as well as different types of rice or buckwheat.

Fish in jelly

Fish are important part of diet and should be eaten frequently therefore there is many fish-based dishes in Polish cuisine. Fish in jelly is another one of them and often seen on Polish Christmas Eve table.

Greek fish

Don’t be confused by the name of this dish. Although Greek fish originates from Greece it has become a traditional Christmas Eve dish as Poles do not eat any meat on Christmas Eve.

Mushroom and cabbage dumplings

Poland has many different types of dumplings to offer, however mushroom and cabbage dumplings are in general well liked throughout all parts of Poland.

Smoked meat

Vegetable salad

Vegetable salad is one of the most common and traditional salads in Poland. Root vegetables such as potato, carrots and parsnip form the basis for this salad.
