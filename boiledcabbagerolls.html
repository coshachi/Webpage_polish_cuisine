Boiled cabbage roll

Boiled cabbage rolls are commonly seen at the dining table in Polish households. They are most commonly stuffed with minced pork and rice, however that can also be stuffed with other kinds of mince meat as well as different types of rice or buckwheat. They are very filling and tasty dinner option.

Bon Appetit!

Difficulty Preparation time: Total time: Portions:
HARD 1 h 00 min 2 h 00 min 5
Ingredients: Sauce ingredients:
large savoy cabbage 2 red peppers
1.5 kg of mince meat (1 kg of beef&pork + Â½ kg of turkey) 1 hot pepper
200g rice 3 garlic cloves
3 eggs 2 medium onions
3 medium onions 4 large strongly red tomatoes (optionally canned tomatoes or tomato puree)
salt, pepper basil, oregano

Method:

  1. LEAVES:
    Remove outer green leaves from the cabbage (they are bitter). Cut out the stump of the cabbage. While brewing in boiling water, remove the leaves one by one using a fork and let them simmer until soft. Cut off the protruding thickened part from the main vein of each leaf.
  2. STUFFING:
    Add 3 eggs, glazed onion, cooked rice, salt and pepper to the meat. Mix all into uniform mass.
  3. SAUCE:
    Add 1 cup of cold salted water into the pot. Into the water add diced onions, garlic, peppers and scalded tomatoes (without skin). Cook for 15 minutes while mixing. Add spices basil, oregano, salt, pepper to taste.
  4. COMBINED TOGETHER:
    Spread out the cabbage leaf on the palm of your hand and add 1-2 tbsp of stuffing. Wrap up the stuffed leaf. Place some cabbage leaves on the bottom of the pot (preferably the ones not suitable for wrapping i.e. damaged ones). Place the cabbage rolls upright in the pot. Cover the rolls with cabbage leaves and pour boiling salted water in. Optionally 1 chicken stock cube might be added. Cook cabbage rolls until soft (about 1 hour). Pour the prepared sauce over the rolls.

    Serve on the plate with a bit of sauce and enjoy.
