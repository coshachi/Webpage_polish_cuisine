Vegetable salad

Vegetable salad is one of the most common and traditional salads in Poland. Root vegetables such as potato, carrots and parsnip form the basis for this salad. Vegetable salad is a special occasion dish and is a centerpiece of any family gathering in a traditional Polish household. This salad is easy to make and most often served for breakfast, supper or as a snack.

Bon Appetit!

Difficulty Preparation time: Total time: Portions:
EASY 0 h 15 min 0 h 20 min 6
Ingredients:
3 carrots
½ parsnip
¼ celery root
5 small salad potatoes
4 eggs
4 medium pickled cucumbers
1 small onion
1 small apple
peas - small can
2 tbsp mustard
4 tbsp mayonnaise
salt, pepper

Method:

  1. Boil vegetables and eggs. Cut cooked vegetables, pickled cucumbers, apple, onions and eggs into cubes. Add peas and mix everything. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add mustard, mayonnaise and mix together.

  2. Place in a bowl, smooth the surface and decorate with fresh parsley.
