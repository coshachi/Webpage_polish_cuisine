Dried fruit compote

Dried fruit compote is one of twelve traditional drinks and dishes consumed on Christmas Eve. However it is also a very refreshing drink in other seasons. Give this one a go at home!

Bon Appetit!

Difficulty Preparation time: Total time: Portions:
EASY 0 h 10 min 3 h 00 min 10
Ingredients:
700g of dry fruit mix: apples, pears, plums, raisins
3 cinnamon sticks
sugar - to taste

Method:

  1. Wash dry fruits.

  2. Place fruits and cinnamon in a tall pot.

  3. Pour cold water into a pot (4 liters).

  4. Cook and sweeten for taste.

  5. Set aside for about 2-3 hours in a cool place.

  6. Mix and pour the dried compote into the jug.

  7. Serve.
